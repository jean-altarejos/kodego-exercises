Enjoy a cup of Coffee with the Princes


Coffee Prince is a coffee and waffle shop located in Seoul, South Korea. We offer coffee and waffles to our customers while being served by our wonderful princes. We want you to enjoy your day by drinking high quality coffee while being served by our pleasant princes.

But first, Coffee!!

If you are looking for homegrown, high quality brewed coffee. You have come to right place. Our coffee is locally harvested from a local farm located in korea. With that in mind we are keen with our coffee beans and ensures to only delivery high quality beans to brew and to serve to you.
We have a wide range of offering from Espresso, to lattes, to frappes etc..Just let our Prince Barista know what you want for the day.

Let's Talk Waffles!

One of our Prince is actually an owner of a mini waffle stand along the road. His belgian waffles are made from heaven. It is light and buttery just like how we want it. Made with love we are sure that our homemmade belgian waffles are going to fill your hearts with joy.
