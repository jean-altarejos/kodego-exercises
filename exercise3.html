Vape Bill lapses into law

https://newsinfo.inquirer.net

MANILA,Philippines - The measure seeking the regulation of vaporized nicotine (vape) and non-nicotine products as well as novel tobacco products - also known as the "Vape Bill" - has lapsed into law. The measure regulates the importation, manufacture, sale, packaging, distribution, use, and communication of vaporized nicotine or vape and non-nicotine products, including novel tobacco products.
